Lesson 13.3 — Exploring setInterval()

Use time-based repetition in AI-driven applications

What is setInterval()?

setInterval() repeatedly executes a function at fixed time intervals (in milliseconds).

Syntax:

setInterval(function, intervalInMilliseconds)
